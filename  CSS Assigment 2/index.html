Fun Facts About Dairy Milk

  • Dairy Milk was first launched in 1905 by Cadbury in the UK.
  • It is famous for its smooth, creamy texture and rich chocolate flavor.
  • Made from fresh milk and the finest cocoa, Dairy Milk is loved in over 100 countries worldwide.
  • It is available in a variety of flavors, including classic milk chocolate, fruit and nut, and caramel.
  • Dairy Milk has been a part of celebrations, gifts, and sweet moments for generations.
